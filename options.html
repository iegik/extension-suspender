Tab Suspender Settings

When enabled, inactive tabs will be automatically suspended to save memory and CPU usage.

Tabs will be suspended after this many minutes of inactivity.

How it works:

  • Inactive tabs are suspended by navigating to about:blank#original-url
  • When you click on a suspended tab, it automatically restores the original page
  • Special pages (chrome://, about:, etc.) are never suspended
  • Click the extension icon to manually suspend/restore the current tab
